About Us

Flowprism is a music player and news site and our mission is to spread our love for music.
Music helps us relax, release stress, and forget about the world for a little bit.
We hope our website provides a space of comfort during this time.

"Music, once admitted to the soul, becomes a sort of spirit, and never dies."





We are located in San Francisco. Please email us at support@flowprism.com
for general inquiries or use the form on our contact page.
